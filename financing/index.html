Financing and Insurance

Making better hearing accessible

Better hearing is an investment in your health, your relationships, and your future. We are here to help you understand your options.

Insurance Benefits

We accept some insurance programs and can help you understand your hearing aid coverage. Many insurance plans now include partial hearing aid benefits, and we work with you to maximize what you qualify for.

Financing Options

We offer financing through trusted healthcare lenders so you can move forward with the hearing care you need. Ask about zero-interest and extended-term options at your consultation.

Health Savings Accounts

Hearing aids and hearing evaluations are typically eligible expenses through Health Savings Accounts (HSA) and Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA). Bring your account information to your appointment.

Veterans Benefits

Veterans may qualify for hearing aid coverage through the VA. We can help you understand your benefits and coordinate care with your VA provider when needed.

Honest Pricing

No sales pressure. Ever.

We built Coastal Hearing Care to be different. That means transparent conversations about pricing, honest recommendations based on your hearing needs (not our margins), and options at multiple technology levels so you can choose what makes sense for you.

Every consultation includes a clear breakdown of what your care will include, what it will cost, and what your financing and insurance options look like. You leave informed, not pressured.
