Our Services

Comprehensive audiology care in Bradenton

From your first hearing evaluation to lifelong follow up, our Doctors of Audiology provide the full range of services you need to hear your best.

Comprehensive Hearing Testing

Our diagnostic hearing evaluations go beyond a simple screen. We measure how you hear speech in quiet and noise, test middle ear function, and identify the type and degree of hearing loss with precision. Every evaluation includes a clear explanation of your results.

Hearing Aid Fittings

We work with major manufacturers to fit hearing aids that match your hearing loss, lifestyle, and preferences. Every fitting includes real-ear measurement to verify the device is programmed correctly for your unique ear.

Hearing Aid Repairs

Our skilled team can diagnose and repair hearing aids from most major manufacturers, from simple cleanings and dome replacements to advanced repairs. We can often fix your devices in-office the same day.

Hearing Aid Refresh Program

Have hearing aids that no longer sound right? Our Refresh program reprograms and optimizes your existing devices to work their best. Often, a Refresh can extend the useful life of your current hearing aids by years.

Custom Hearing Protection

Custom-fit hearing protection for musicians, industrial workers, motorcyclists, hunters, and swimmers. We take precise ear impressions in-office and craft protection tailored to your ears and use case.

Tinnitus Evaluations

Ringing, buzzing, or hissing in your ears? Our tinnitus evaluations identify possible causes and give you a personalized management plan, including sound therapy, hearing aid options, and lifestyle strategies.

Earwax and Cerumen Removal

Safe, professional cerumen removal by trained providers. We use gentle, evidence-based techniques to remove blockages, restore hearing clarity, and protect the health of your ear canal.

Ear Impressions and In-Ear Monitor Scans

Custom ear impressions and digital ear scans for in-ear monitors, hearing protection, sleep plugs, and swim molds. Whether you are a stage performer or a side sleeper, we get the fit right.

Telehealth Appointments

When appropriate, we offer telehealth appointments for adjustments, follow-ups, and consultations. Save the drive and connect with your provider from home.

What to Expect

Your first visit at Coastal Hearing Care

We designed your first visit to be thorough, comfortable, and educational. Here is what happens:

  1. Case history so we understand your concerns, lifestyle, and hearing goals.
  2. Ear inspection using video otoscopy so you can see inside your own ears.
  3. Comprehensive diagnostic testing including pure tones, speech testing in quiet and noise, and middle ear function.
  4. Results review where we explain your audiogram and what it means for your day-to-day hearing.
  5. Personalized recommendations based on your results, priorities, and budget.
